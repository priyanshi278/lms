About LibGuard LMS

LibGuard is a modern Library Management System designed for efficiency and safety.

Key Features:

  • Role-Based Access: Separate portals for Admins and regular Users.
  • Catalog Management: Easily manage books, movies, and categories.
  • Automated Circulation: Seamless issuing and returning of items.
  • Fine Management: Automatic fine calculation for late returns.
  • Comprehensive Reports: Deep insights into library activity and membership.
